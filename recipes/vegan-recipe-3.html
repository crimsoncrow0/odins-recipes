Vegan Recipe 3

Description

Vegan fish bites made from Cauliflower. These amazingly tasty little bites are incredible, they have the texture and taste of white fish and are really simple to make.

They're made from Caluiflower too, so there really cheap!

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower
  • Beer (for the batter)
  • Some spices
  • Seaweed type stuff (for the fishy flavouring)

Steps

  1. Blend the cauliflower and spices
  2. Shape into little balls and put in the fridge
  3. Mix the flour and beer to make the batter
  4. Dip the balls in the batter
  5. Fry until golden brown and crispy
  6. Serve with fires and mushy peas
